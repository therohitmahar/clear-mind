### FAQ Section
**Q1**: I've never taken therapy before. What should I expect in the first session?
**A1**: Arrive 10 minutes early to complete any necessary forms and relax before your session. Avoid eating a heavy meal right before your appointment. Wear comfortable clothing, and communicate any health conditions or concerns to your therapist.

**Q2**: How do I know if I really need therapy?
**A2**: If something is affecting your daily life, relationships, work, or emotional well-being, therapy can help. You don't have to be in a "crisis" to seek support. Therapy is also for self-growth, clarity, and emotional balance.

**Q3**: Is everything I share kept confidential?
**A3**: Yes. Your privacy is very important to us. Everything discussed in sessions remains confidential, except in rare situations where safety is at risk (which will always be discussed with you).

**Q4**: How many sessions will I need?
**A4**: It depends on your goals and concerns. Some people benefit from short-term therapy (6–8 sessions), while others prefer longer support. We will regularly review your progress together.

**Q5**: How do I book a session?
**A5**: You can book a session through our website, WhatsApp button, or by directly contacting ClearMind Counseling. Our team will guide you through the next steps. We are open for both online sessions and in-person sessions.
