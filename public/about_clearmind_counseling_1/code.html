About
ClearMind Counseling

Founded by Janhavi More and Vikas Kamble to make therapy feel compassionate, clear, and deeply human.

A counseling practice built on empathy, structure, and trust

ClearMind Counseling was established in 2023 by Janhavi More and Vikas Kamble with a simple belief: therapy is not about fixing you, but understanding you.

The practice was built to offer compassionate, non-judgmental support that helps people challenge unhelpful patterns and build emotional stability that does not depend on outside circumstances.

Real change happens when you change, not only when the world changes. That philosophy still guides every session, conversation, and recommendation at ClearMind.
